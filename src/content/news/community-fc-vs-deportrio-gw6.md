## Community FC 3-9 Deportrio — Gameweek 6 Report

Deportrio demolished Community FC 3-9 at Baller Arena in Gameweek 6 in an emphatic display. Community FC came into the match sitting 12th in the table, while Deportrio were 4th.

## First Half

The first half saw the Plus One Game Changer activate at the 12th minute, producing 3 goals. Deportrio established control early with an attacking display that set the tone for the match.

## Second Half

1-on-1 Game Changer kicked in at the 27th minute, adding 5 goals to the game. Deportrio cruised to a 6-goal victory.

## The Game Changer

The Game Changer period erupted into life with 8 goals across both activations. The 1st half Plus One produced 3 goals. 1-on-1 added 5 more in the 2nd half.
