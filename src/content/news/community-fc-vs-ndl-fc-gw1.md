## Community FC 2-7 NDL FC — Gameweek 1 Report

NDL FC demolished Community FC 2-7 at Baller Arena in Gameweek 1 in an emphatic display. Community FC came into the match sitting 11th in the table, while NDL FC were 1st.

## First Half

The first half saw the 3Play Game Changer activate at the 12th minute, producing 3 goals. NDL FC established control early with an attacking display that set the tone for the match.

## Second Half

The Line Game Changer kicked in at the 27th minute, adding 1 goal to the game. NDL FC cruised to a 5-goal victory.

## The Game Changer

The Game Changer brought 4 goals across both activations. The 1st half 3Play produced 3 goals. The Line added 1 more in the 2nd half.
