## Rukkas FC 3-8 Prime FC — Gameweek 2 Report

Prime FC demolished Rukkas FC 3-8 at Baller Arena in Gameweek 2 in an emphatic display. Rukkas FC came into the match sitting 12th in the table, while Prime FC were 4th.

## First Half

The first half saw the Plus One Game Changer activate at the 12th minute, producing 4 goals. Prime FC established control early with an attacking display that set the tone for the match.

## Second Half

1-on-1 Game Changer kicked in at the 27th minute, adding 2 goals to the game. Prime FC cruised to a 5-goal victory.

## The Game Changer

The Game Changer period erupted into life with 6 goals across both activations. The 1st half Plus One produced 4 goals. 1-on-1 added 2 more in the 2nd half.
