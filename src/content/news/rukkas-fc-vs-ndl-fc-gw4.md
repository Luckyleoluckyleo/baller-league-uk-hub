## Rukkas FC 4-6 NDL FC — Gameweek 4 Report

NDL FC defeated Rukkas FC 4-6 at Baller Arena in Gameweek 4. Rukkas FC came into the match sitting 11th in the table, while NDL FC were 1st.

## First Half

The first half saw the Plus One Game Changer activate at the 12th minute, producing 2 goals. NDL FC established control early with an attacking display that set the tone for the match.

## Second Half

The Fairplay Game Changer kicked in at the 27th minute, adding 1 goal to the game. NDL FC cruised to a 2-goal victory.

## The Game Changer

The Game Changer brought 3 goals across both activations. The 1st half Plus One produced 2 goals. The Fairplay added 1 more in the 2nd half.
